Our Story

Jaxson and Emma met in 6th grade when Emma switched into Jaxson's elementary school. They played soccer together every recess, and Jaxson sometimes even passed her the ball!

For most of middle school, they had all of their classes together. In high school, they had the same friend group, and Jaxson took Emma to his first high school dance: Junior Homecoming. It was magical.

They never dated in high school, and saw each other few times while Emma attended BYU, but they were always drawn to each other. They went on their missions, came home within a month of each other, and had their first kiss a week later.

They've been together over a year, and will soon be united for eternity!
